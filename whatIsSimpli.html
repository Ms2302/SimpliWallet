What is SimpliWallet?

This Guide

SimpliWallet is a desktop based Cryptocurrency wallet that allows you to store your tokens and send/recieve them to and from other addresses. Unlike other wallets, SimpliWallet is designed to help all users with their investing adventure whether you are a beginner or an experienced user. SimpliWallet offers a wide range of ERC-20 tokens that the user can send and recieve.

Our Mission

A simpler way to manage your money

SimpliWallet's mission is to encourage new users to delve deep into the world of Cryptocurrency and use it to improve their lives through the gain that can be found. There is a clear gap in the market for truely user friendly crypto wallets, especially for those who are just starting out, SimpliWallet is here to fill that gap by providing a product that is easy to use and offers enough features to keep users around.
