Sending and recieving tokens

Sending tokens

To send tokens you hold in SimpliWallet, simply press the "send" button on your table of holding. This will present a popup menu asking you to enter the recieving address of the wallet you wish to send the tokens to, then enter the amount you'd like to send a simply press "send". The tokens will then leave your wallet and be deposited in the wallet specified. Your application will then update your portfolio accordingly.

Recieving tokens

To deposit tokens with SimpliWallet, first click the "deposit" button on your wallet page. This will produce a blue popup that will allow you to copy your address to clipboard by pressing the "copy" button. From here you can send your tokens to that address from an external wallet.

Never share your Seed Phrase!
